Unsafe Structures – Nairobi

January 2026: A 16-story building in South C collapsed, killing multiple people. Four extra illegal floors were suspected to have been approved via bribes.

80% of Nairobi buildings considered unsafe
🔒

Data Breach – Worldcoin

Global scrutiny in 2024–2025 over insecure iris scan collection. Kenya's High Court deemed Worldcoin's biometric collection unlawful, mandating deletion of all Kenyan data.

Millions of biometric records at risk
📱

Data Breach – Local Loan Apps

Kenyan mobile loan apps accessed users' contacts and photos, leading to harassment and public shaming. ODPC fined companies like Whitepath and Mulla Pride millions.

KES 9.3M+ in fines issued
🌊

Pollution – Kenya

Despite the 2017 plastic bag ban, Nairobi faces severe plastic pollution, clogging rivers, causing floods, and breeding mosquitoes. The Nairobi River is heavily polluted with toxic metals.

Chromium & lead levels 500% above safe limits
⚔️

Kenyan Volunteers – Fighting for Russia

Reports have emerged of individuals from Kenya participating in the Russian conflict, raising questions about international recruitment, personal risk, and global impact.

Numbers unclear, growing national concern

Workplace Hazards

Construction collapses and factory incidents highlight poor safety standards, lack of PPE, dangerous machinery, and chemical hazards.

How Mehit Works

Upload evidence, map controls across frameworks (ISO 45001, Data Protection Act, NEMA regs, etc.), implement & track — all in one platform.

1 Upload Evidence & Discover Risks

Upload photos, PDFs, inspection reports, sensor data. AI maps controls to multiple frameworks (safety, environmental, data protection) and flags gaps before incidents occur.

  • AI-powered risk detection
  • Multi-framework compliance mapping
  • Real-time gap analysis

2 Map & Implement Controls

Automatically map uploaded evidence to standards (e.g., OSHA-equivalent, Kenya Data Protection Act, NEMA waste rules). Deploy automated checklists, safe scaffolding protocols, encryption, waste tracking.

  • Automated compliance mapping
  • Custom control implementation
  • Framework-agnostic approach

3 Track, Report & Prove Compliance

Live dashboards show control status, evidence coverage, risk scores across frameworks. Generate audit-ready reports instantly — visible to regulators, communities, insurers.

  • Real-time compliance tracking
  • Audit-ready reporting
  • Stakeholder transparency

4 Improve & Adapt Continuously

Machine learning identifies patterns, predicts emerging risks, and suggests improvements. Systems evolve with changing regulations and environments.

  • Predictive risk analytics
  • Continuous improvement
  • Regulatory change adaptation

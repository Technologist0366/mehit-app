Harm Happens Silently — and Often Preventably

Recent tragedies show what happens when compliance fails. These are not accidents — they are preventable system failures.

Unsafe Structures – Nairobi, Jan 2026

A multi-storey building under construction in South C collapsed on January 2, 2026, killing at least two people and trapping others. Illegal floors, poor supervision, and regulatory lapses turned a site into a tragedy.

Biometric Data Breach – Worldcoin Kenya

High Court ruled Worldcoin's iris scan collection unlawful (2025). Ordered permanent deletion of all Kenyan biometric data under ODPC supervision — exposing thousands to privacy risks without consent or safeguards.

Loan Apps Privacy Violations

ODPC fined companies like Whitepath and Mulla Pride millions for accessing contacts/photos without consent — leading to harassment, shaming, and fraud against vulnerable borrowers.

Ongoing Pollution – Nairobi River & Beyond

Despite the 2017 plastic ban, rivers remain clogged with waste, toxic metals (chromium, lead), and flood risks. Industrial fires and poor enforcement continue to poison communities and ecosystems.

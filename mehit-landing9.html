Unsafe Structures – Nairobi 🏗️⚠️

January 2026: A 16-story building in South C collapsed, killing multiple people. Four extra illegal floors were suspected to have been approved via bribes.

80% of Nairobi buildings are considered unsafe due to corruption, poor materials, and lack of supervision.

Data Breach – Worldcoin 🔒🔓

Global scrutiny in 2024–2025 over insecure iris scan collection.

Kenya’s High Court deemed Worldcoin’s biometric collection unlawful, mandating deletion of all Kenyan data under ODPC supervision.

Data Breach – Local Loan Apps 📱🛡️

Kenyan mobile loan apps accessed users’ contacts and photos, leading to harassment and public shaming.

ODPC fined companies like Whitepath and Mulla Pride millions for violating data protection laws.

Pollution – Kenya 🌊🗑️

Despite the 2017 plastic bag ban, Nairobi faces severe plastic pollution, clogging rivers, causing floods, and breeding mosquitoes.

The Nairobi River is heavily polluted with toxic metals like chromium and lead.

Pollution – Global (Russia) 🏭🔥

Industrial waste fires continue in Russia and other regions, causing deaths, toxic air, and environmental degradation — a silent global crisis.

Workplace Hazards – Mombasa & Nairobi ⚒️🩹

Construction collapses and factory incidents highlight poor safety standards, lack of PPE, dangerous machinery, and chemical hazards.

Workers remain at high risk due to poor regulatory compliance.

How Mehit Works

Upload evidence, map controls across frameworks (ISO 45001, Data Protection Act, NEMA regs, etc.), implement & track — all in one platform.

1. Upload Evidence & Discover Risks

Upload photos, PDFs, inspection reports, sensor data. AI maps controls to multiple frameworks (safety, environmental, data protection) and flags gaps before incidents occur.

2. Map & Implement Controls

Automatically map uploaded evidence to standards (e.g., OSHA-equivalent, Kenya Data Protection Act, NEMA waste rules). Deploy automated checklists, safe scaffolding protocols, encryption, waste tracking — prevention becomes routine.

3. Track, Report & Prove Compliance

Live dashboards show control status, evidence coverage, risk scores across frameworks. Generate audit-ready reports instantly — visible to regulators, communities, insurers — trust through transparency.
